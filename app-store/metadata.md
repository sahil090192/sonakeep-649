## Description
SonaKeep is a local-first gold inventory and portfolio tracker built for people who want a calmer, more private way to keep track of physical gold holdings.

Use it to catalogue jewelry, coins, bars, and other gold items in one place, monitor current estimated value using live gold pricing, and keep sensitive numbers discreet with Privacy Mode and biometric lock on supported devices.

### What you can do with SonaKeep
- Add and organize gold items by category
- Track weight, purity, purchase price, date, location, and notes
- View current estimated portfolio value
- See derived valuation across 24K, 22K, 18K, and 14K purity levels
- Search, filter, and sort your holdings
- Hide monetary values with Privacy Mode
- Protect app access with biometric lock on supported devices

### Built to feel private and practical
SonaKeep currently stores your holdings and settings on your device rather than requiring an account. The app can fetch public gold pricing data for valuation and labels clearly whether rates are live, cached, or fallback.

### Important notes
- SonaKeep is a record-keeping and valuation utility, not financial advice
- Valuation depends on current pricing data and purity-based derivation
- Device-level backup remains your responsibility in the current version
